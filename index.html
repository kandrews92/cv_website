I am a Ph.D student in experimental condensed matter physics at Wayne State University.

My main scientific interest is to understand the intrinsic properties of two-dimensional materials. My research is currently focused on atomically thin transition metal dichalcogenides, such as tungsten diselenide and molybdenum disulfide.
